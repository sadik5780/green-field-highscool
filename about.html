We Are Greenfields English High School

The learning environment in the classroom is to adapt CBE methodology (Competence Based Education), which is also aimed at teachers being sensitive to the various needs of the different learners, in particular to students with learning challenges and slow learners. This provides an atmosphere where the learning interaction inside & outside the classroom is done with a lot of understanding and sensitivity.

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance, inclusion and excellence.
